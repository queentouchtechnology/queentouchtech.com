Table Tap: Smart Restaurant Order Management

21 Oct 2025 | Queen Touch Technology

Table Tap is a cutting-edge restaurant order management app developed by Queen Touch Technology. Designed to streamline restaurant operations, it simplifies order taking, kitchen management, and billing processes.

With Table Tap, waitstaff can take orders directly on a mobile or tablet device, send them instantly to the kitchen, and generate bills seamlessly. The app ensures real-time order tracking, reduces errors, and improves overall customer satisfaction.

Table Tap supports menu customization, special offers, table management, and analytics to help restaurant owners make data-driven decisions. Whether managing a small café or a multi-branch restaurant, Table Tap optimizes efficiency and enhances the dining experience.
