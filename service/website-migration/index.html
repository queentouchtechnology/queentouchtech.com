1. Secure Website Migration

A successful website migration requires careful planning and execution. Our team securely transfers your website files, databases, configurations, and essential resources while maintaining your website's functionality and data integrity.

  • Securely migrate website files and databases.
  • Preserve website functionality and configurations.
  • Minimize downtime during the migration process.

2. Hosting and Server Migration

Whether you're moving to a faster hosting provider, upgrading your server infrastructure, or switching to a cloud environment, QTT helps you migrate your website smoothly. We configure the new hosting environment and carefully move your website to ensure a stable and reliable transition.

  • Migrate websites between hosting providers.
  • Move websites to new servers and cloud environments.
  • Configure domains, DNS, SSL, and hosting settings.

3. CMS and Platform Migration

Changing your content management system or website platform can improve flexibility, performance, and scalability. Our CMS migration services help businesses move their websites and content to a new platform while maintaining important data, website structure, and functionality.

4. SEO-Friendly Website Migration

Website migration can affect search engine rankings if it is not handled correctly. At QTT, we follow an SEO-friendly migration process that focuses on preserving your website's search visibility and minimizing potential ranking issues.

  • Preserve important URLs and website structures.
  • Implement appropriate redirects to prevent broken links.
  • Check metadata, sitemap, and indexing configurations.
  • Monitor website performance after migration.

5. Website Migration Testing and Optimization

After migration, we thoroughly test your website to ensure everything works as expected. From page functionality and forms to mobile responsiveness, SSL configuration, and website speed, our team checks the critical elements before and after the migration.

Our post-migration checks help identify potential issues early and ensure your website delivers a smooth experience for users and search engines.
