Intelligent Attendance Tracking

Automate daily attendance through GPS-based check-ins, QR code scans, or biometric integration. Ensure accuracy, reduce manual errors, and monitor presence in real time across multiple locations.

Smart Leave Management

Apply, approve, and manage leaves digitally with transparent workflows. Office Pulse automatically syncs leave types, carry-forward balances, and holiday calendars for seamless HR operations.

Shift & Work Hour Scheduling

Create flexible shift patterns and automate work-hour calculations. Managers can assign shifts, rotate rosters, and view live attendance summaries directly from the dashboard.

Employee Directory & Lifecycle

Maintain a centralized employee database — roles, departments, designations, and joining details. Office Pulse makes onboarding and lifecycle tracking efficient and transparent for HR teams.

Payroll Integration

Link attendance and leave data directly to payroll. Generate accurate salary slips with automatic late mark adjustments and overtime calculations — reducing HR workload significantly.

Real-Time HR Analytics

Gain actionable insights on attendance, punctuality, and workforce performance. Interactive dashboards help managers make informed, data-driven HR decisions instantly.

Automated Alerts & Notifications

Receive smart alerts for late arrivals, leave approvals, and shift updates. Keep your workforce informed and connected through real-time push notifications.

Secure Cloud Data & Scalability

Hosted on encrypted cloud servers, Office Pulse ensures enterprise-grade security and seamless scalability — making it perfect for teams of any size, from startups to large enterprises.

Frequently Asked Questions

1 - What is Office Pulse, and who can use it?

Office Pulse is a complete HR and Attendance Management App developed by Queen Touch Technology. It helps organizations of all sizes track attendance, manage leaves, automate payroll, and analyze employee performance — all from one centralized platform.

2 - How does GPS and QR attendance tracking work?

Employees can mark attendance either by scanning a unique QR code or via GPS-based check-in from authorized office locations. This ensures authenticity, eliminates proxy attendance, and provides real-time tracking for HR teams.

3 - Can Office Pulse integrate with payroll systems?

Yes, Office Pulse integrates attendance and leave data directly with payroll modules. It automatically calculates working hours, late marks, and overtime — making salary generation faster and error-free.

4 - Does Office Pulse support multiple locations or shifts?

Absolutely. Office Pulse supports multi-location attendance and shift scheduling. Admins can define flexible rosters, manage remote teams, and view consolidated reports across all branches.

5 - Is employee data secure in Office Pulse?

Yes. Office Pulse uses end-to-end encryption and secure cloud servers to store all employee data safely. Only authorized users can access information, ensuring full compliance with modern data protection standards.

6 - Can employees access Office Pulse from mobile devices?

Yes, Office Pulse is fully mobile-friendly and available on Android. Employees can mark attendance, request leaves, check shift timings, and receive updates directly from the mobile app.
