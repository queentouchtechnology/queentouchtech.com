Key Features

Table Tap brings together powerful tools to simplify restaurant operations — from order-taking to billing — ensuring faster service, higher accuracy, and a smoother dining experience.

Digital Table Ordering

Allow waiters to take and update orders directly from mobile devices. No missed notes, no confusion — every table stays organized with real-time digital tracking.

Kitchen Order Display System (KDS)

Orders are sent directly to the kitchen as soon as they’re placed. Chefs can view, update, and mark dishes as ready — eliminating paper tickets and communication delays.

Multi-User Access & Roles

Set up different roles for waiters, kitchen staff, and admins. Control access, monitor team activity, and ensure seamless coordination across your restaurant.

Instant Kitchen-Waiter Communication

Receive live notifications for order updates, modifications, and ready alerts. Keep staff connected and reduce confusion during peak hours.

Sales & Performance Analytics

Track daily orders, sales trends, and staff performance through an intuitive analytics dashboard designed for restaurant insights and growth.

Cloud-Based Order Management

Access your restaurant data securely anytime, anywhere. Orders, reports, and customer info are safely stored and synced in the cloud.

Smart Notifications

Get notified instantly for new orders, kitchen updates, completed bills, or pending tasks — keeping everyone in sync effortlessly.

Integrated Billing & Printing

Generate and print accurate bills instantly at the table or counter. Reduce waiting time and improve customer experience with automated billing integration.

Waiter Interface

The Waiter Interface is built to make order-taking fast and error-free. Waiters can browse menu items, take multiple table orders, and send them directly to the kitchen in seconds.

With digital tables and smart syncing, every order update — from adding an item to marking it served — is instantly reflected across the system. This enables restaurants to eliminate miscommunication, reduce waiting time, and improve service quality.

Kitchen Interface

The Kitchen Interface provides a clear, real-time view of all active orders directly from waiters. As soon as an order is placed, it appears on the Kitchen Display Screen (KDS) for preparation.

Chefs can update cooking status, mark orders as ready, or send back notifications to the waiter panel instantly. This real-time synchronization keeps every table and dish perfectly on track.

Frequently Asked Questions

1 - What is Table Tap, and who can use it?

Table Tap is a smart restaurant order management app developed by Queen Touch Technology. It helps restaurants, cafés, and food courts digitize the entire process — from taking orders to sending them to the kitchen and generating bills — all in real time.

2 - How does Table Tap manage orders between the waiter and kitchen?

Waiters take customer orders directly on their mobile devices. Once placed, the order is instantly displayed on the Kitchen Display Screen (KDS) for preparation. Chefs can mark items as “ready,” and waiters receive instant notifications — ensuring smooth and fast communication without manual slips.

3 - Can I customize the menu and categories in Table Tap?

Yes! You can fully customize your digital menu — add, edit, or delete categories, food items, and prices anytime. Menu updates are reflected instantly across all connected devices for both waiters and kitchen staff.

4 - Does Table Tap support billing and printing?

Absolutely. Table Tap comes with an integrated billing system that generates digital or printed bills instantly. It supports Bluetooth and LAN printers, ensuring a seamless experience for dine-in, takeaway, and online orders.

5 - Can Table Tap work offline?

Table Tap primarily works online for real-time updates and cloud syncing. However, an offline mode is under development to allow order taking and billing in low-connectivity environments, syncing data automatically when the internet is restored.

6 - Is my restaurant’s data secure with Table Tap?

Yes. Data security is a top priority at Queen Touch Technology. All restaurant information — including menu data, orders, and billing records — is encrypted and securely stored in cloud servers with restricted access control.
