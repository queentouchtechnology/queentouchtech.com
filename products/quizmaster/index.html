Examiner Account

The Examiner (also called Test Creator) is the core role for educators, trainers, and institutions. With Quiz Master, examiners can create exams, manage participants, post study materials, and evaluate performance — all within one simple interface.

They can organize study papers and timed exams, assign tests to specific groups or individuals, and review detailed answer sheets for each attendee. This account empowers institutions with data-driven insights and real-time analytics.

View Examiner Guide

Attendee Account

The Attendee (or Participant) represents students, employees, or learners. Each attendee can register using an Examiner ID or be added directly by the Examiner to access tests and materials.

Once logged in, attendees can take exams, view their scores, and analyze performance. They also receive real-time notifications about upcoming tests, results, and updates from their Examiner.

Frequently Asked Questions

1 - What is Quiz Master, and who can use it?

Quiz Master is an all-in-one digital exam and assessment platform designed for educators, institutions, corporate trainers, and students. It helps create, schedule, and evaluate exams effortlessly — all through a single app.

2 - How can I create and manage exams?

Examinees (test creators) can log in, create study or exam papers with different question types (MCQ, true/false, fill-in-the-blank, descriptive), assign them to attendees or groups, and set start and end times for each test.

3 - Can attendees review their answers after an exam?

Yes! After completing an exam, attendees can access their detailed answer sheet and compare their responses with the correct ones. This helps them identify strengths and areas for improvement immediately.

4 - Does Quiz Master support notifications and announcements?

Absolutely. The app includes a built-in notice board and push notification system, allowing Examinees to share important updates, announcements, and results with all attendees instantly.

5 - Is my data safe and private?

Yes. Quiz Master follows strict security measures for data protection. All information including user credentials, exam details, and scores are securely stored and encrypted in our servers.

6 - Is Quiz Master available offline?

Currently, Quiz Master works online to ensure live result tracking and analytics. However, offline test mode is planned for a future update to support exams in low-connectivity areas.
