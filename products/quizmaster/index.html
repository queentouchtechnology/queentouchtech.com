How Quiz Master Works

Quiz Master simplifies the entire assessment process — from test creation to results analysis. Built for educators, institutes, and corporate trainers who value efficiency and accuracy.

Creators (Examinees) can build and assign exams, while Learners (Attendees) can attempt tests, get instant feedback, and track progress in real time — all in one integrated platform.

Sign Up

Join as an Examinee or Attendee using your organization code.

Create Papers

Design custom papers with MCQs, true/false, fill blanks or descriptive questions.

Schedule Exams

Assign papers, define time limits, and choose attendees or groups.

Evaluate & Analyze

Instant scoring for MCQs, detailed reports for descriptive answers, and visual performance charts.

All steps are integrated seamlessly

From exam design to real-time feedback. Quiz Master empowers both learners and educators with transparency and speed.

Key Features

Quiz Master brings together powerful features that make test creation, participation, and evaluation fast, flexible, and transparent.

Smart Paper Management

Create reusable study and exam papers with MCQs, descriptive, true/false, and fill-in-the-blanks — all organized for easy access.

Timed & Scheduled Exams

Set exam start/end times, duration limits, and automatic submissions for total control and fairness in every test.

Attendee & Group Management

Add attendees individually or by group. Manage permissions and track progress across all participants in real time.

Communication Tools

Built-in notice board, push notifications, and support ticket system ensure seamless interaction between Examinees and Attendees.

Answer Sheet Review & Comparison

Attendees can review their answers after the exam, compare with correct responses, and learn from mistakes for continuous improvement.

Scoreboards & Analytics

Get real-time leaderboards, average scores, and performance charts that highlight top performers and improvement areas.

Instant Notifications

Stay updated with exam alerts, results announcements, and important notices in real time.

Reusable Papers & Templates

Reuse question sets and papers across multiple exams, saving time while ensuring consistency in evaluation.
